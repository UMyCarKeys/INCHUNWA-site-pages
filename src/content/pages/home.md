## Reclaiming Ancestral Marks, Together

The Inchunwa Project is a community-rooted initiative dedicated to the revitalization of Southeastern Indigenous tattooing traditions. Through our podcast, educational resources, and direct community work, we are building a model where community members are active participants in sustaining the cultural, relational, and ceremonial foundations of ancestral marking practices.

Our work is grounded in education, cultural care, and the belief that the reclamation of ancestral marks belongs to the whole community — not just practitioners, but every person who carries the stories, relationships, and responsibilities that give these marks their meaning.
